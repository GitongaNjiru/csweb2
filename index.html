We Provide the following Services

Revenue Collection And Management System

Our Revenue Collection System is a digital solution designed to help clients efficiently track, manage, and collect revenue. By automating processes, it reduces leakages, improves transparency, and ensures seamless revenue collection across multiple streams

Hospital Management Information System

Our Hospital Management System streamlines patient management, integrates billing and insurance, and enhances departmental communication, improving efficiency, coordination, and patient care throughout the healthcare process.

Human Resource Information System

Our Human Resource and Payroll Management System centralizes employee records, automates payroll, ensures compliance with statutory regulations, integrates with county systems, and provides real-time workforce analytics.

Electronic Document Management System

Our Electronic Document Management System streamlines records management for county governments by digitizing files, ensuring secure storage, enabling quick retrieval, and supporting regulatory compliance.

Enterprise Resource Planning System

Our ERP system integrates county operations, streamlines workflows, enhances financial management, ensures data accuracy, improves service delivery, and provides real-time analytics for informed decision-making.

Fleet And Fuel Management System

Our Fleet and Fuel Management System optimizes county operations by tracking vehicle usage, monitoring fuel consumption, reducing costs, improving maintenance, and enhancing transparency for efficient resource management.
